Software Steering Council (SSC) Representative

Each official subproject in Jupyter gets a single Software Steering Council representative. Voilà' representative is elected by the active team members. In the Voilà subproject, our representative is elected to a one-year term.

Voilà's SSC representative is Martin Renou
